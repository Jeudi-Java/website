How Cohortly works

Step 1

Sign up with your university email

Verify your identity with a campus email so the community stays safe and student-only.

Step 2

Tell us what you’re here for

Friends, study buddies, projects, startups, jobs, or all of the above — customize your goals.

Step 3

Match, chat, build, get hired

Meet people, join teams, start something new, and get discovered by employers watching the network.
